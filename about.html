Our Vision & Mission

Manoved is on a mission to normalize depression and other mental health challenges by providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality mental healthcare to everyone. We focus on improving the mental well-being of young India for a healthier society and a brighter future.

Our Services & Innovations

We offer expert therapy sessions, self-help resources, educational blogs, and interactive wellness programs—all at a price everyone can afford. Our carefully selected therapists are among the best in their fields, and with upcoming ML and AI integrations, we’ll personalize your therapy experience like never before.
